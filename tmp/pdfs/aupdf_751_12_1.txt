Overall attitude and impression of the Leasing Professional:
Steven has a fun and upbeat personality. I’m sure he addresses the students' needs well.
Primary Closing Technique used by the Leasing Professional:
Stephen made it clear before during and at the end of the visit that spaces available for the fall are limited. He made sure I knew about
deadlines for the current promotion as well as how to apply quickly. Additionally, he provided his contact information for any future questions or
help I might need in the application process.
Why you would / would not have leased based on the presentation:
I absolutely would have leased for a potential child of mine. It’s a well-kept facility with a wonderful manager. I could see a student enjoying the
amenities as well as the proximity to the university. The staff that I met were all friendly and available to help if needed.
Strengths of the Leasing Professional's presentation:
Stephen did a good job showing all of the amenities and a model unit quickly, but without feeling rushed. He was responsive to any questions I
had but clearly took the lead to show me everything available. Although Steven is still learning some of the ins and outs of the computer
software, overall, he appeared knowledgeable about the property.
Leasing Professional's opportunities to improve:
As Stephen is with this complex longer, it will be good for him to become more comfortable with the computer software and confident in the
rates available.
Overall summary of experience and service provided:
Overall, this was a great experience. Well furnished, clean, fresh smelling apartments. Friendly leasing staff. Quality amenities. And fantastic
location.
